Welcome to Emunyo Contractors Limited

Emunyo Contractors Limited is a registered construction company aiming at producing quality civil and building engineering works. It being a destination for all contractors, the company is goal oriented and purposive in delivering civil and building works for clients.


Emunyo Contractors Limited was incorporated on 22nd October 2013 under the Companies Act, Cap.110 of the Laws of Uganda and thereof, commenced its works as at the date of incorporation. It is private company limited by shares. The profits earned are shared in accordance to the share capital contributed. There are two shareholders in the company i.e. Mr. Emunyo Abdul (Managing Director) and Ms. Mbabazi Ronah (Executive Director) with the former being the largest shareholder.
