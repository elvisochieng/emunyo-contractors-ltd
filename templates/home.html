Welcome to Emunyo CONTRACTORS Ltd.

Emunyo Contractors Limited was incorporated on 22nd October 2013 under the Companies Act, Cap.110 of the Laws of Uganda and thereof, commenced its works as at the date of incorporation. It is private company limited by shares. The profits earned are shared in accordance to the share capital contributed. There are two shareholders in the company i.e. Mr. Emunyo Abdul (Managing Director) and Ms. Mbabazi Ronah (Executive Director) with the former being the largest shareholder.

OUR SERVICES

Some of the services offered by the company.

Steel works
Steel cutting and bending of iron bars (Y25, T20, Rings) has been done for tying columns, beams, ramp, stair cases and slab as indicated below.
Concrete works
Casting of already mixed concrete supplied by Arab Contractors limited to cast the slab and then used two concrete mixers to cast the beams and columns.
Carpentry works
Decking, shattering beams and columns, ramp, making column boxes, raising the pvc net for mitigating dust on the site, roofing, as supervised by the company.
Administrative/ management works
Supervision of the site was done by the project technical team such as the project manager, Eng.Emunyo Abdul, the structural engineer.
Plumbing and electrical works
Water pipes and electric pipes, sewer pipes were installed in the structure through the slab and walls basing on the utility plan and approved by the architect and the client.
